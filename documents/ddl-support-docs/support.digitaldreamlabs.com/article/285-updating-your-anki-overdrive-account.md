# Updating your Anki OVERDRIVE account {#updating-your-anki-overdrive-account .title}

[](javascript:window.print() "Print this article"){.printArticle
aria-label="Print this Article"}

OVERDRIVE and OVERDRIVE:Fast & Furious Edition allow - and encourage -
you to create an Online Player Account to save the following game stats
online: 

1.  **Campaign Progress **
2.  **Rank**
3.  **Trophies**
4.  **Coins ** 

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## Sections:

**[Creating an account](#h_534930443471548070510176) \|\|  [Changing
your Driver Name](#h_9841263981548070516873) \|\|  [Resetting your
password](#h_9370576961271548070521825) \|\|  [Changing your email
address](#h_9595621551651548070529400) \|\|  [Deleting your
account](#h_841753562011548070536025)**
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Unless you are playing offline / have your internet turned off on your
device the app will ask you to create an online player account. This
account allows you to log into your account from different devices
whilst protecting your precious game progress in case you deleted the
app or changed your device.  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## Creating an account {#h_534930443471548070510176}

When setting up OVERDRIVE / OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition, the app
will guide you through the process of setting up your first account.
**Additional accounts can be created by:**

1.  Selecting the *Burger* Menu in the top left corner of your app home
    screen
2.  Clicking on \"Settings\"
3.  Selecting \"Profile\"
4.  Choosing \"Sign Up / Log In\" to create a \"New Driver\" 

### Email address requirements

-   Min. 5 characters
-   Max. 100 characters
-   Must contain an @ symbol
-   Must follow the format x@x.x where x is one or more [**ASCII
    characters**](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~pattis/15-1XX/common/handouts/ascii.html)
    (characters 0-127) 

### Username requirements

-   Max. 100 characters
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## How to change your Driver Name? {#h_9841263981548070516873}

While it is not possible to change an existing Driver Name via the app
you can create a new Online Player account with the preferred name. The
Profile menu (in the Settings section) allows you to toggle between the
different Online Player accounts that are linked to the same email
address. 
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## [How to reset your password? ]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large} {#h_9370576961271548070521825}

If you can\'t remember your password or get a \"wrong password\" message
you can follow these steps: 

1.  Select the *Burger* Menu in the top left corner of your app home
    screen
2.  Click on \"Settings\"
3.  Select \"Profile\" 
4.  Click on the \"Sign Up / Log In\" button (log out if you are
    currently logged into the Online Player Account) 
5.  Click on \"Forgot Driver Name or Password?\" and enter the email
    address that is linked to your account to receive a password-reset
    email. 
6.  Follow the steps as outlined in the email to reset your password 

### Password requirements

-   Min. 8 characters
-   Max. 32 characters
-   Must be composed of [**ASCII
    characters**](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~pattis/15-1XX/common/handouts/ascii.html)
    (characters 0-127)
-   Must have an upper case letter
-   Must have a lower case letter
-   Must have a number
-   [**UTF-8**](https://www.fileformat.info/info/charset/UTF-8/list.htm)
    characters not accepted
-   Emojis not accepted
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## [How to change your email address? ]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large} {#h_9595621551651548070529400}

1.  Select the *Burger* Menu in the top left corner of your app home
    screen
2.  Click on \"Settings\"
3.  Select \"Profile\" and log in with your Driver Name and Password
4.  Click on the cog icon next to \"Log out\"  
5.  Verify your password (see above section if you have forgotten your
    password) 
6.  Select \"Change Email Address\" 
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## [How to delete your Online Driver Account? ]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large} {#h_841753562011548070536025}

If you wish to delete your Online Driver account please contact Customer
Care and provide your Driver Name as well as the matching email address
so that our team can delete your account. 
:::
