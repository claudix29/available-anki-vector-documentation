## ls my device compatible with Vector and the Vector Robot companion app?

[Vector and the Vector Robot companion app are designed to work with
popular modern devices. ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

##  

[Please Note: If your device cannot find and download the Vector Robot
companion app in the Apple App Store, Google Play store, or Amazon
Appstore, then your device will not work with Vector. Please read
[here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/85-compatible-devices-further-information)
for more information.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

##  

[When selecting a mobile device to use with Digital Dream Labs products,
please consider the following important guidelines:
]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Minimum requirements]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Validated devices]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Operating systems]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

##  

[Digital Dream Labs tests and publishes a list of devices with operating
systems that we know to work well with Digital Dream Labs products.
These lists are actively updated as we improve our products. Due to
differences among devices, some devices that meet or exceed the minimum
requirements may not work well with Vector.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
:::

## Minimum Requirements

### [Operating System]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [ iOS 10]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [ Android 5 (Lollipop)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [ Fire OS 5]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

### [Hardware]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [ A WiFi internet router that broadcasts in at least 802.11n 2.4GHz
    channels]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
:::

## If Vector is autonomous, why do I need to use a device with Vector? Why do I need the Vector Robot companion app?

##  

[While Vector is an autonomous robot (you can interact with him using
your voice and he will automatically update himself when a software
update is available), the Vector Robot companion app is required
to:]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [ Successfully set up Vector]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [ Associating your Vector with your Anki
    Account]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [ Configuring Vector to join WiFi networks and setting basic
    settings]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [ Help you learn how to interact with
    Vector]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [ Manage Face Recognition and Photos]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
