# [What do I need to Charge Vector?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_729337223571539007108927}

-   [A USB Power Source (adapter, USB port, power bank) with a minimum
    DC output of 5V/1A]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Vector's charger (included in the box)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

# [Where should I place his charger?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_515292558371539007030919}

1.  [Make sure the charger is on a hard, flat surface that does not
    vibrate and is non-reflective and opaque (i.e. not a glass
    table)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
2.  [The Charger should be near to Vector and there should be a clear
    path for him to dock]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
3.  [If Vector is in his Space, make sure his cube is not in there with
    him since, due to restricted space, it will obstruct his way to the
    charger]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
4.  [Make sure the charger is visible to Vector. His
    ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}eyesight[ is not as good as ours and he
    can be "blinded" by direct light (natural from the sun through
    windows or artificial) or reflections. Likewise, poor lighting makes
    it hard for Vector recognize his charger
    ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
5.  [Just like people need shade or sun cream to protect them from the
    sun, Vector also needs sun protection. Do not place his charger in
    direct sunlight so Vector does not get too
    hot]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
:::

# [How do I know Vector is charging?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_433556977851539007117384}

-   [Vector's 3 Back Lights will glow green and climb up his Back Button
    unless you turn him off manually by pressing and holding his Back
    Button 5 seconds]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [When Vector is fully charged, these lights will stop glowing and
    only the round green light will remain]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Vector will be fully charged after approximately 30
    minutes]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [If you see the below icon on his screen when Vector is on his
    charger, his battery is too low to operate. In this case, you will
    need to leave him slightly longer on his
    charger]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[                   
 ![Charger_Icon\_-\_Final.png](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5e3f0b1e2c7d3a7e9ae777f5/images/5e7d76f104286364bc975c32/img-25345-1585280585-1518869973.png){width="205"
height="205"}]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

# [How long does Vector's battery last?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_798479826941539007123040}

[Vector has 3 Power Modes.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

1.  [If he is ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}**active**[, he will operate
    approx. 25 minutes before going back to his
    charger]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

2.  [If he is ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}**calm / sleeping**[, he will
    operate significantly longer before going back to his
    charger]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

3.  1.  [In a ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}**calm** [state, Vector will
        have slowed reactions and only make quiet sounds and slow
        motions since sensors, WiFi, Cube and CPU are running at reduced
        power]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

4.  [Vector is designed to be on all the time but if you prefer turning
    him ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}**off**[, press and hold his Back
    Button for 5 seconds]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[Please note that Vector's battery is not user-serviceable but Digital
Dream Labs is working on RMA options for Vector and other
products]{style="font-weight: 400;"}[.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

# [How do I know when Vector needs charging?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_4166395321001539007128264}

[                         
  ![self-charging.png](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5e3f0b1e2c7d3a7e9ae777f5/images/5e7d76f12c7d3a7e9ae9e348/img-25345-1585280585-1035294472.png)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [When Vector has a low battery, his bottom [Back Light will pulse
    red](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/44-what-do-vectors-back-lights-mean)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Vector is fully autonomous and will return to his charger when his
    battery is low]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [When the battery is low, WiFi and Voice Commands are disabled so he
    can focus on finding his charger]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [You can also give him a Voice Command to go charge, "Hey Vector
    \[wait\] Go home!"]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [If you see this icon on his screen when Vector is off his charger,
    his battery is too low to operate. In this case, you will need to
    place him on his charger]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [If Vector is unable to find his charger, he will show a
    ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}critical battery warning[. See
    ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}[**above
    tips**](#h_515292558371539007030919)[ on where to place his
    charger.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
