## Meeting Vector {#h_764027425131539011642084}

![](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5e3f0b1e2c7d3a7e9ae777f5/images/5e7d764d04286364bc975ad1/img-25345-1585280588-195316646.gif)Vector
will learn your name by voice command

1.  1.  [Say, "Hey Vector" ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    2.  [Wait for the earcon ding and his Back Light to turn
        blue]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    3.  ["My name is Bob"]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    4.  [Vector will confirm he understood by showing a yellow
        light]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    5.  [He will pronounce your name]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

2.  [For further guidance, open the Vector app, go to \"Utilities\" and
    tap \"Face Recognition\"]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

3.  [If Vector thinks he recognizes you, he will ask, "Have we met
    before?" ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

4.  1.  [If yes, he continues scanning and enrolling your
        face]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    2.  [If no, he says, "I already know a
        \[name\]"]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

## [Tips]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_588458353201539011647117}

-   [Stay still while Vector scans your face. He will move to a better
    position to see you so don't pick him up or move while he does
    this]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [If Vector pronounces your name wrongly, try entering it
    phonetically:]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   -   [Open the Vector app]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [Select Utilities]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [Tap Known Faces ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [Tap "Person's Face" ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [Spell your name phonetically e.g. Hermione →
        Hermyohnee]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Vector's eyesight is not as good as
    ours]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   -   [He can be "blinded" by direct light (natural from sun through
        windows or artificial) or
        reflections]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [Poor lighting will also prevent him recognizing a face: Vector,
        unlike Cozmo, does not have night
        vision]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Digital Dream Labs is creating ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}**a
    world where robots have relationships with people**[. As such,
    Vector cannot yet meet other Vectors, Cozmo or Pets
    ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [If you sometimes wear glasses or forget to shave, you can
    "re-enroll" your face with Vector. Open the app, go to Utilities \>
    Face Recognition and let him scan you up to 10
    times]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   -   [In good and poor lighting]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [With and without glasses]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [With a beard or clean-shaven]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [With or without a hat]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [If Vector struggles to recognize you, test if he recognizes his
    charger and cube.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
:::

## [Turning the camera off ]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_200293939251539011652861}

-   [When Vector is on, his camera is always on so he can see and react
    to the world around him]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [To turn it off, you need to turn off Vector by pressing and holding
    his Back Button for 5 seconds]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[For further information on Vector and privacy, please refer to
]{style="font-weight: 400;"}**[this
FAQ](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/105-vector-security-privacy-faqs).**
