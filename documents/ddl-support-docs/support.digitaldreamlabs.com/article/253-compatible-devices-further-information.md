## What is the general rule for device compatibility? {#h_928998309941548413969961}

The vast majority devices that can download our App from their
respective app store are likely to support all aspects of OVERDRIVE and
OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition.

If you can use your device to search for, find, and download the App in
the Apple App Store, the Google Play store, or the Amazon Appstore that
device is very likely to be compatible with OVERDRIVE and OVERDRIVE:
Fast & Furious Edition.

Please be aware that because of the significant number of different
models, variants, and manufacturing standards of available devices, we
can only assure the compatibility of devices that we actively test
internally. **A list of compatible devices can be found
[here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/255-compatible-devices-overdrive-fast-furious-edition).**
:::

::: sections
## What if my device cannot find and download the App? {#h_8590172361931548413988289}

You've used your device to search for, but cannot find and download the
App from the Apple App Store, the Google Play store, or the Amazon
Appstore locations.

This means that your device is known to not support OVERDRIVE and
OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition. Either it does not meet a specific
requirement, or we've specifically disabled it because there
were problems that we could not fix or workaround.
:::

## Our apps are not supported running on devices that are:

-   Jailbroken, rooted, or running custom ROMs
-   Running a Beta operating system
-   Installed on external memory (SD Cards, USB, etc.)

**Our apps are only supported on devices running officially released
builds provided by their manufacturer and installed on internal
memory.**

## [Other devices that should be compatible]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large} {#h_825986613351548414002072}

While we do not actively test the following devices, if you own one and
it is running at least iOS 9.3.5, Android 5 (Lollipop), or Fire OS
5.3.1.0, it will likely work as expected with OVERDRIVE or OVERDRIVE:
Fast & Furious Edition.

These devices are not being actively tested, but we are confident that
they should work well with OVERDRIVE or OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious
Edition and should be able to Host games.

## What are the Minimum Requirements? {#h_9897249143891548414013353}

In general, for a device to potentially work, it needs to meet or exceed
both of these Software and Hardware Requirements.

###  

### Software

-   iOS 9
-   Android 4.4 (Kit Kat)
-   Fire OS 4 (Android 4.4.2)

## Is there anything else to try if a device cannot find the App in any app store? {#h_7456430844401548414018568}

If you are using an Android device or an Amazon Kindle Fire OS device
and they are not listed anywhere on this page and your unable to
download the OVERDRIVE app from the App Store, you can try using the 
**[Android app (apk) for unsupported
devices](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/244-download-the-android-apk){target="_blank"
rel="noopener noreferrer"}**. We currently are planning on making a
similar option available for OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition.

**This .apk file will install on both Android and Fire OS devices.**

Please note that we try to update and revise this article as often as
possible, given the significant number of shipping and new devices,
Android operating system versions with manufacturer customizations, the
specifics in this article may become out of date.
