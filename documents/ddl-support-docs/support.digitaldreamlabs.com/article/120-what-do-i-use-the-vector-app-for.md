# What do I use the Vector app for? {#what-do-i-use-the-vector-app-for .title}

[](javascript:window.print() "Print this article"){.printArticle
aria-label="Print this Article"}

[The key thing you'll need the app for is setting up Vector, getting him
connected to your home WiFi network for the first time.
]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[Once the app gets Vector going, you'll want to keep it around for
things like seeing what he's doing, checking the photos you have him
take, reviewing the people he's met, or getting suggestions on how to
use a voice command. ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[The Vector app is there to help you when you need help with Vector. See
below for what you can use the app for after getting
started.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

## [Update settings]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[Open the Vector app and go to Settings in order
to:]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Update your Account]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Check Cube status]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Allows you to force a software update if Vector does not update
    automatically]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Check networks]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Change Vector's eye color]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Turn the volume up or down]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[Open the Vector app and go to Settings then tap Preferences in order
to:]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Change Vector's location]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Change the time zone]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Select if you prefer units in the metric or imperial system\... or
    a mix of both]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Change the language (remember, it's best to choose depending on
    your accent, not on your physical
    location)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[Any changes in the Settings are sent from the App to the robot and from
the robot to the Cloud (excluding photos, and volume and eye color). See
]{style="font-weight: 400;"}[this
FAQ](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/112-how-to-reset-erase-and-restore-vector)
[for further information about resetting, erasing or restoring
Vector.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
