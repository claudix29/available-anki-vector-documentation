# What Do Vector\'s Back Lights Mean? {#what-do-vectors-back-lights-mean .title}

[](javascript:window.print() "Print this article"){.printArticle
aria-label="Print this Article"}

Vector has a series of lights on his back. These Back Lights change
color to show Vector\'s current state. Learn below what Back Lights
Vector displays and what they mean.
