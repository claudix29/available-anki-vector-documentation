# Compatible Devices: Further Information {#compatible-devices-further-information .title}

[](javascript:window.print() "Print this article"){.printArticle
aria-label="Print this Article"}

This article explains in greater detail device compatibility information
with the Vector app (also known as the Vector Robot app). [**It
supplements this** **[List of Compatible
Devices](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/83-compatible-devices){target="_blank"
rel="noopener"}**. ]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

## **[What are the Minimum Requirements?]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large}**  {#h_3098182452251543922381075}

In general, for any device to potentially work with the Vector app
(Vector Robot app), it needs to meet or exceed both of these Software
and Hardware requirements.

###   {#section .u-text-bold}

### Software {#software .u-text-bold}

-   iOS 10 or iOS 11
-   Android 5 (Lollipop) through Android 9 (Pie)
-   Fire OS 5

###   {#section-1 .column}

### Hardware {#hardware .column}

-   A WiFi internet router that broadcasts in at least 802.11n 2.4GHz
    channels
:::

::: sections
## **[What is the general rule for device compatibility?]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large}** {#h_5866719112661543922386491}

The vast majority devices that can download the Vector app (Vector Robot
app) from their respective app stores are likely to support all aspects
of the app.

If you can use your device to search for, find, and download the Vector
app in the Apple App Store, the Google Play store, or the Amazon
Appstore that device is very likely compatible with Vector app.

[**Please be aware** that because of the significant number of different
models, variants, and manufacturing standards of available devices,
Digital Dream Labs can only assure the compatibility of devices that we
actively test internally. **A list of compatible devices can be found**
[here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/83-compatible-devices).]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## **[What if my device cannot find and download the Vector app?]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large}** {#h_1510473353151543922392172}

If you have used your device to search for, but cannot find and download
the Vector app (Vector Robot app) from the Apple App Store, the Google
Play store, or the Amazon Appstore locations, this means that your
device is known to not support the Vector app.

Either it does not meet a specific requirement, or we've have
specifically disabled it because there were problems that we could not
fix or workaround.
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## **[What devices does Digital Dream Labs know do not work?]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large}** {#h_8495090403511543922400396}

[**Note:** By its nature, this list is incomplete and subject to change
at any time and without notice.]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[ ]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[All of the following devices are known to Digital Dream Labs to have
failed compatibility testing with the Vector app.  They are
fundamentally incompatible with our products. Digital Dream Labs
strongly recommends that you do not attempt to use these products with
ours.]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[ ]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

  -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ---------------------------
  [Any Tablet or Device with any x86, x86-64, Intel, AMD, or Atom based Processor]{.wysiwyg-color-black}   
  [ ]{.wysiwyg-color-black}                                                                                [ ]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
  -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ---------------------------

[ ]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[**Digital Dream Labs apps are not supported running on devices that
are:**]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

-   [Jailbroken, rooted, or running custom ROMs]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [Running a Beta operating system]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [Installed on external memory (SD Cards, USB,
    etc.)]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[ ]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[**Digital Dream Labs apps are only supported on devices running their
manufacturer\'s officially released builds and installed on their
built-in internal memory.**]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## **[Other devices that should be compatible]{.wysiwyg-font-size-large}**  {#h_6517387723821543922405195}

While Digital Dream Labs does not actively test the following devices,
if you own one and it is running at least iOS 10.3.3, Android 5.1
(Lollipop), or Fire OS 5.4.1.0, it will likely work as expected with the
Vector app.

These devices are not being actively tested, but we are confident that
they should work well with the Vector app.

  --------------------- --------------------------
  OnePlus OnePlus One   Samsung Galaxy Note Edge
  Sony Xperia Z3         
  --------------------- --------------------------
