### Is my device compatible with Cozmo and the Cozmo app? {#is-my-device-compatible-with-cozmo-and-the-cozmo-app .u-text-bold .u-pt1}

::: sections
[Cozmo and the Cozmo app are designed to work with popular modern
devices.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[**Please Note:** If your device cannot find and download the Cozmo app
in the Apple App Store, Google Play store, or Amazon Appstore, then your
device will not work with Cozmo. Please read
[here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/134-compatible-devices-further-information)
for more information.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[When selecting a mobile device to use with DDL products, please
consider the following important guidelines:
]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Minimum requirements]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Validated devices]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Operating systems]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[DDL tests and publishes a list of devices with operating systems that
we know to work well with DDL products. These lists are actively updated
as we improve our products. Due to differences among devices, some
devices that meet or exceed the minimum requirements may not work well
with Cozmo.]{style="font-weight: 400;"} 
:::

::: sections
## [Notes]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[This list of validated devices may change at any time and without any
notice. We will do what we can to keep this list as up to date as
possible.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

###   {#section-6 .u-text-bold .u-pt1}

[\* These manufacturers have devices with the same or similar names to
the devices we test and publish here, but they are not compatible with
DDL products. For example, while we successfully test the Samsung Galaxy
S5 with the Cozmo app, the Samsung Galaxy S5 Neo or Samsung Galaxy S5
Mini are not tested and may not work.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

###   {#section-7 .u-text-bold .u-pt1}

[We also have specifically disabled some devices because they have known
issues with the Cozmo app or are not standard. For example, the Cozmo
app does not support any Tablet or Device that uses an x86, x86-64,
intel, AMD, or Atom based Processor. Several Huawei and Xiaomi devices
do not work well with the Cozmo app. Please read
[here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/134-compatible-devices-further-information)
for more information.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
