## What is the general rule for device compatibility?

[The vast majority of devices that can download our App from their
respective app store are likely to support all aspects of
Cozmo.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[If you can use your device to search for, find, and download the App in
the Apple App Store, the Google Play store, or the Amazon Appstore that
device is very likely to be compatible with
Cozmo.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[[Please be aware that because of the significant number of different
models, variants, and manufacturing standards of available devices, DDL
can only assure the compatibility of devices that we actively test
internally. ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}**A list of compatible devices
can be
found **[here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/135-compatible-devices)[.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
:::

::: sections
## What if my device cannot find and download the App? {#h_813700961281537888310281}

[You've used your device to search for, but cannot find and download the
App from the Apple App Store, the Google Play store, or the Amazon
Appstore locations.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[This means that your device is known to not support Cozmo. Either it
does not meet a specific requirement, or we've have specifically
disabled it because there were problems that we could not fix or
workaround.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
:::

## Huawei Lite Devices {#h_4328396273881538137151043}

[Some Huawei \"Lite\" devices have performance and disconnection issues
running the Cozmo app. Based upon our tests, we have determined that
some of these devices are not compatible with the Cozmo app and have had
to manually excluded them from installing the app via the Google Play
Store.]{.wysiwyg-color-black style="font-weight: 400;"}

## Sony Xperia Devices

[Some Sony Xperia devices crash upon running the Cozmo app. Based upon
our tests, we have determined that these devices are not compatible with
the Cozmo app and have had to manually excluded some of them from
installing the app via the Google Play Store.]{.wysiwyg-color-black
style="font-weight: 400;"}

[**DDL apps are not supported running on devices that
are:**]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

-   [Jailbroken, rooted, or running custom ROMs]{.wysiwyg-color-black
    style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Running a Beta operating system]{.wysiwyg-color-black
    style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Installed on external memory (SD Cards, USB,
    etc.)]{.wysiwyg-color-black style="font-weight: 400;"}

[**DDL apps are only supported on devices running officially released
builds provided by their manufacturer and installed on internal
memory.**]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

## Other devices that should be compatible {#h_848250199451537888321261}

[While DDL does not actively test the following devices, if you own one
and it is running at least iOS 9.3.5, Android 5 (Lollipop), or Fire OS
5.6, it will likely work as expected with
Cozmo.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[These devices are not being actively tested, but we are confident that
they should work well with Cozmo.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

  -------------------------------------------------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
  [Apple iPhone 7 Plus]{style="font-weight: 400;"}   [Samsung Galaxy S5 (excluding Mini / Neo range)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
  [Apple iPad Air 2]{style="font-weight: 400;"}      [Samsung Galaxy S4 (excluding Mini range)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
  [Apple iPad Air]{style="font-weight: 400;"}        [Samsung Galaxy Note Edge]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
  [Apple iPad 3]{style="font-weight: 400;"}          [Galaxy Note 3 (excluding Neo range)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

## What are the Minimum Requirements? {#h_964048755501537888327063}

[In general, for a device to potentially work, it needs to meet or
exceed both of these Software and Hardware
Requirements.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[Software]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [iOS 9]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Android 5 (Lollipop)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Fire OS 5]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

## Is there anything else to try if a device cannot find the App in any app store? {#h_148300674101538137158926}

[If you are using an Android device or an Amazon Kindle Fire OS device
and they are not listed anywhere on this page and your unable to
download the Cozmo app from the app store, you can try using the
]{style="font-weight: 400;"}[**COZMO: APK for Unsupported
Devices**](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/228-download-the-android-kindle-fire-apk){target="_blank"}[.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

###  

This .apk file will install on both Android and Fire OS devices.

[Please note that we try to update and revise this article as often as
possible, given the significant number of currently available and new
devices, Android operating system versions with manufacturer
customization, the specifics in this article may become out of
date.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
