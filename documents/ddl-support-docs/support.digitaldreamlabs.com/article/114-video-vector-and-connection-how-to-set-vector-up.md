## Quick overview of setup {#getting_started}

1.  Please ensure that you have [purchased a
    subscription](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/344-all-about-memberships).
    If you are a Kickstarter or early investor, you may use an existing
    license by emailing us at support@digitaldreamlabs.com.
2.  The Vector app finds and connects to Vector via a signal that he
    emits (Vector will **not** be listed in your smart device\'s WiFi or
    Bluetooth settings)
3.  The app then allows Vector to scan for available home WiFi networks
4.  Once Vector has connected to the preferred WiFi network, he connects
    to the internet to access Digital Dream Labs\'s servers
5.  Once connected successfully, Vector will download the latest
    firmware version from Digital Dream Labs\'s server
6.  Vector will then reboot and connect to your device via WiFi to
    complete the setup

## [Requirements for setting up Vector]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_107409751538839253375}

-   [A]{style="font-weight: 400;"} [**compatible
    device**](https://support.anki.com/hc/en-us/articles/360007328894-VECTOR-Compatible-Devices)[
    to run the Vector app -- required for
    setup]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [A Vector Account -- valid email address and account activation
    required]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [802.11n 2.4 GHz WiFi network connected to the Internet via a WiFi
    Access Point (NAT router)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [USB Power Source (adapter, USB port, power bank, etc.) with min.
    5V=1A output]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

1.  [For a pdf version of the Vector Quick Start Guide (QSG), see
    ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}[**here**](https://support.anki.com/hc/en-us/articles/360010005093-Quick-Start-Guide)

2.  [Unbox Vector, his charger and his Cube on a clean, well-lit surface
    or place Vector in his Space]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

3.  [Download the Vector app, open it and follow the in-app prompts. The
    app is designed to guide you through the steps but we have listed a
    summary of the steps below: ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    1.  [The password requires min. 8 characters, at least 1 lowercase
        letter, at least 1 upper case letter, at least 1
        number]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    2.  [Note: the password does not allow special
        characters]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

4.  1.  [Vector only supports English language: select American, British
        or Australian English]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    2.  [Connect the USB cable on Vector's charger to a compatible power
        source and place Vector on his
        charger]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    3.  [Enter your Date of Birth in Month-Date-Year format. Note: you
        must be 18+ to have a Vector account
        ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    4.  [Enter your email address to create a new Vector Account or if
        you have an OVERDRIVE account, or created an account on
        Anki.com, log in with your existing Username (not your email
        address)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    5.  [Create a password]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    6.  [Depending on your region, select if you want to allow Digital
        Dream Labs anonymized usage data collection to improve
        services]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    7.  [Select if you would like to be signed up to the
        newsletter]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    8.  [Check your inbox for the activation email and click "Activate
        Account" (check your Spam folder if you can't see it or resend
        the email from the Vector app. Note if you resend the activation
        email, you need to click the link from the newest** **email to
        avoid getting sent to a 404) ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    9.  [Make sure your device's Bluetooth and WiFi are
        enabled]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    10. [The app will now search for Vector to connect to him
        wirelessly]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

5.  [When the App finds Vector, tap on
    "connect"]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

6.  1.  [press Vector's Back Button twice (press hard on top of the LED
        strip - you will hear it click, see video below) and enter the
        6-digit pin displayed on his screen into the
        app]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

7.  [Once the Vector app has connected wirelessly to the robot, he will
    allow you to select and connect to your home WiFi access point
    ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

8.  1.  [If you want to connect Vector to a hidden WiFi network, tap,
        "Don't see your WiFi network?" and use the "Enter the SSID for
        your WiFi Network" dialog]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

9.  [Once WiFi connection has been established, Vector will ask
    permission to download the latest version of his Operating System
    (OS)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

10. [If the update is successful, the robot will reboot and the app will
    reconnect - Vector will display a Flashing "V in a Phone
    icon"]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

11. Now set the robot's Preferences for its "Location" (manually type it
    into the field), time zone, temperature and distance units (metric,
    imperial, or a mix), and finally clock display - these are important
    for Vector to operate correctly

12. [Tap "Start" to complete setup and you will be guided to the in-app
    tutorial - we suggest you explore the app to get familiar with your
    new companion!]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
