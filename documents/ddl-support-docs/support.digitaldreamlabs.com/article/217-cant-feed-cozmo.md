## 1) Feeding Cozmo for the first time in the Nurturing Tutorial

[Please follow these steps to help feed Cozmo for the first
time:]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

-   [**Turn up your device\'s volume in the Cozmo app** so you can
    clearly hear the music and all of the audible cues when interacting
    with Cozmo]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [**Remove the plastic guard form underneath Cozmo\'s chin** and any
    other plastic supports]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [**Pull the clear plastic tabs out of each cube** - the cubes will
    flash for a few seconds]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [**Let Cozmo detect his cubes:**]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
    -   [If Cozmo successfully found his cubes, you\'ll see 3 check
        boxes]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
    -   [If Cozmo cannot detect and connect to any cubes, there will not
        be a green checkbox (see [**Section
        2**](#h_481582469551530721775625) for
        troubleshooting)]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[![New_Pull_Tab.png](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5e3f0b1e2c7d3a7e9ae777f5/images/5e7d77242c7d3a7e9ae9e391/img-2828-1585280649-29318814.png)]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

-   [**Shake a connected cube to energize it** (connected cubes will
    display a single blue cycling light). You will hear a \"charging\"
    sound from your device\'s speaker. Each of the cube\'s 4 lights will
    get brighter, glowing one at a time until they all become a SOLID
    Blue]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[![Cube_Loaded_Highlight.png](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5e3f0b1e2c7d3a7e9ae777f5/images/5e7d77252c7d3a7e9ae9e392/img-2828-1585280649-1308439878.png)]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

-   [**Place the cube down and allow Cozmo to
    feed**]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

## 2) I can\'t feed Cozmo during the Nurturing Tutorial

[Please perform the following steps:]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

1.  [**Reset the cubes** to check the batteries are
    working:]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
    1.  [unscrew the lid]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
    2.  [wait 10 seconds]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
    3.  [screw the lid back on]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
    4.  [the cube should flash red\>green\>blue]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
2.  [**Move all 3 cubes to another room** to stop Cozmo from trying to
    connect to them]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
3.  [**Force close the Cozmo app** (the cubes should still be in the
    other room)]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
4.  [**Relaunch the Cozmo app.** [When the app reconnects to Cozmo, it
    will pick up where the app left off at feeding
    Cozmo]{.s1}]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
5.  [**Give Cozmo a quick snack** to bypass energizing a cube: ]{.s1
    .wysiwyg-color-black}

[![Reconnect_Cubes.png](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5e3f0b1e2c7d3a7e9ae777f5/images/5e7d772504286364bc975c73/img-2828-1585280649-1127809955.png){width="558"
height="315"}

## 3) I can\'t Feed Cozmo after completing the Nurturing Tutorial

[If your cubes are not working, firstly follow the troubleshooting in
[**this
FAQ**](https://support.anki.com/hc/articles/115000425448-COZMO-How-to-troubleshoot-Cozmo-s-Cubes).]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[If your cubes are working, give Cozmo a quick
snack:]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

1.  [**tap the \"x\"** on the top left of the Feed Cozmo
    screen]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
2.  [**tap the \"settings\" icon** on the top right of the main
    menu]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
3.  [**swipe to the \"cube status\"** screen]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
4.  [**tap \"get help\"**]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
5.  [**tap \"next\" until slide 6** \"Cozmo too hungry to
    play?\"]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
6.  [**tap \"quick snack\"** then \"ok\" and exit the
    settings]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

[Then:]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

1.  [**check Cozmo\'s lift is working correctly** by stacking cubes in
    Explorer Mode]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
2.  [**check Cozmo is recognizing objects
    correctly**:]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
    1.  [clean the around area around Cozmo's camera with a cotton swab
        and a little rubbing alcohol (surgical
        spirit)]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
    2.  [in Explorer Mode, check that Cozmo can visually ID a face and
        can \"greet\" that person]{.wysiwyg-color-black}

## 4) I don\'t have an \"x\" on the top left of the Feeding screen

[The \"x\" will only appear once you\'re completed the onboarding
tutorial. If the \"x\" still does not appear after that, it is a
transitory issue. Please try again later or uninstall and reinstall the
Cozmo app.]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
