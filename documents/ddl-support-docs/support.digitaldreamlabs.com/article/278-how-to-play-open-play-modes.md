# How to play Open Play modes {#how-to-play-open-play-modes .title}

::: sections
## How to play Race

-   [This game mode is pure Racing, spotlighting your driving
    skills]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   No Weapons allowed
-   No Support Items allowed
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## How to play Battle

-   [Disable your opponents using weapons to earn
    points]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [Use your Support Items to support your
    attack]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [First to earn set amount of points wins the
    game]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## How to play Battle Race

-   [First across the finish line wins the game]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [Use your Weapons and Support Items to slow down your
    opponents]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [No restrictions]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## How to play Takeover

-   [You\'ll need at least 2 Supercars]{style="line-height: 1.5;"}
-   [You can only have 1 Supertruck (no more, no
    less)]{style="line-height: 1.5;"}
-   When the game starts, the Supertruck is initially controlled by
    \"AutoPilot\"
-   Each player attacks the lone Supertruck, trying to disable it
-   When a player successfully \"takes over\" the Supertruck, they have
    access to all of its exciting weapons
-   Each Supercar that is disabled by the Supertruck counts as 1 point
    for the player in control
-   Supercars disabled by Supercars do not earn points
-   The first player who successfully uses the Supertruck to disable
    indicated number of Supercars wins the game
-   That winning Supercar driver earns Experience Points for their
    Supercar
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## How to play Time Trial

-   [Time Trial is unique in that there will only be 1 car on the track
    during the Time Trial]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [During the track scanning, all participating cars will scan the
    track together]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [After the scanning is completed, only 1 car will line up at the
    start finish line]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [All remaining participants will be parked off to the side of the
    track]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [After each racer finishes their Time Trial, you'll be asked to
    place the next car on the track]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [To win in Time Trial, you must set the lowest overall
    time]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [**Hint:** Time Trial does not use any weapons and a special Boost
    item is available during the game]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
-   [Be careful using Boost, if you use it too close to a corner, your
    vehicle can spin out and slow you down]{.wysiwyg-color-black}
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## How to play King of the Hill

-   The objective of King of the Hill is to be the \"King\" for a set
    amount of points
-   Each point = 1 second of time
-   When the match starts, no one is King
-   The first person to disable someone will become the King
-   Once you've become the King, your score will start increasing until
    you win or you're disabled by one of your opponents
-   Once you are disabled, your score will stop increasing
-   Disable the King to start increasing your score again
-   The first one to reach the set point limit wins
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## How to play One Shot

-   Aim of the game is to disable your opponents with One Shot
-   All players have same weapon: \"The Golden Sniper\"
-   Shoot strategically, your weapon loses energy when deployed and
    takes time to reload
:::
