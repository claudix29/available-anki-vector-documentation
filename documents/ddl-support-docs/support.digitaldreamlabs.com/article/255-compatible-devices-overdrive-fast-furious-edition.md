## ls my device compatible with Anki OVERDRIVE or Anki OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition apps?

##   {#section .u-text-bold .u-pt1}

Please Note: If your device cannot find and download the OVERDRIVE or
OVERDRIVE: Fast & Furious Edition app in the Apple App Store, Google
Play store, or Amazon Appstore, then your device will not work with
OVERDRIVE. Please read
[**here**](https://support.anki.com/hc/articles/115006643947-Compatible-Devices-further-information)
for more information.

##   {#section-1 .u-text-bold .u-pt1}

When selecting a mobile device to use with our products, please consider
the following important guidelines:

-   Minimum requirements
-   Validated devices
-   Operating systems

We test and publish a list of devices with operating systems that we
know to work well with our products. These lists are actively updated as
we improve our products. Due to differences among devices, some devices
that meet or exceed the minimum requirements may not work well with
OVERDRIVE.

validating (using to develop and test) OVERDRIVE and the OVERDRIVE apps.

## Notes

ª Our app is not optimized for all of the features and functionality
available in newer devices. For example, the OVERDRIVE apps do not take
advantage of the higher display resolutions or aspect ratios on devices
like the iPhone X and Google Pixel 3 XL.

##   {#section-7 .u-text-bold .u-pt1}

\^ These devices are not Fully Compatible because they [can only join an
Anki OVERDRIVE game hosted by another compatible
device](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/253-compatible-devices-further-information).
They cannot be a host that allows other devices to join a game.

\* These manufacturers have devices with the same or similar names to
the devices we test and publish here, but they are not compatible with
Anki products. For example, while we successfully test the Samsung
Galaxy S5 with the OVERDRIVE apps, the Samsung Galaxy S5 Neo or Samsung
Galaxy S5 Mini are not tested and may not work.

##   {#section-8 .u-text-bold .u-pt1}

We also have specifically disabled some devices because they have known
issues with the OVERDRIVE apps or are not standard. For example, the
OVERDRIVE apps do not support any Tablet or Device that uses an x86,
x86-64, intel, AMD, or Atom based Processor. Please read
**[here](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/253-compatible-devices-further-information)**
for more information.

The list of compatible or tested devices may change at any time and
without any notice.
