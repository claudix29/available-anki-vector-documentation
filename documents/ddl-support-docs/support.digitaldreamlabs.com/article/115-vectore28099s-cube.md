## [What battery does the cube use?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_916401561331539002421350}

-   [Alkaline N / E90 / LR1 1.5 Volt
    batteries]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Do not use A23 batteries - these will damage the
    Cube]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
-   [Do not use rechargeable batteries - these will damage the
    Cube]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## [How long does the battery last?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_904779067551539002425973}

-   [Batteries come pre-installed in Vector's
    Cube]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Once the pull-tab is removed, the battery will start to lose
    charge]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [The battery will last 20-40 hours of gameplay depending on how much
    Vector interacts with the Cube]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [To test if the battery is working, reset the
    cube:]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   -   [unscrew the lid from the cube]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [wait for 10 seconds]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [screw the lid back on]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [the cube should flash red\>green\>blue if the battery is
        fine]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
:::

------------------------------------------------------------------------

::: sections
## [How do I change the battery?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_704656899181539002339381}

1.  [Remove the cube's screw using a cross head screwdriver and open the
    cube's cover]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
2.  [Remove and ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}[safely
    dispose](//support.digitaldreamlabs.com/article/88-product-disposal-information)[
    of the old battery]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
3.  [Insert a new battery, with the positive end toward the Cube Door
    that you removed.]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
4.  [Replace the cover and screw the cover back down (not too tightly
    which can damage the cube)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

[![Battery_Diagram.png](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5e3f0b1e2c7d3a7e9ae777f5/images/5e7d76ee2c7d3a7e9ae9e341/img-25345-1585280584-1033400553.png)]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

## [How does Vector interact with the cube?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_193239252811539002440687}

-   [Vector\'s cube is like a pet\'s favorite toy, his possession, and
    from a technical standpoint, it\'s a landmark for him as he
    navigates and explores the area]{.wysiwyg-color-black
    style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Vector interacts with his cube on two levels:]{.wysiwyg-color-black
    style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   -   [Visually: He recognizes the cube by its marking (even without a
        battery) ]{.wysiwyg-color-black style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [Wirelessly (the cube will show in your device\'s Bluetooth
        settings, while Vector will not): He connects wirelessly to the
        cube if it is nearby. If a cube is too far away or the battery
        is low or dead, Vector may not be able to sync with
        it]{.wysiwyg-color-black style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [Vector will tap the cube if he's so inclined]{.wysiwyg-color-black
    style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [You can ask Vector to do a trick such as Roll the cube, Pounce or
    Pop a Wheelie]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [You can ask Vector, "Hey Vector \[wait\] where is your
    cube?"]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
:::

## [Why does Vector ignore his cube?]{style="font-weight: 400;"} {#h_193239252811539002440687}

-   [Vector will not connect to his cube if he is sleeping. Wake him up
    by getting his attention with "Hey
    Vector!"]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [If Vector's cube is in the Vector Space, he will ignore
    it]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   [If the cube has a low battery or the battery is dead, he will not
    be able to connect to it]{style="font-weight: 400;"}

-   -   [You may try to force-sync the cube by opening the Vector app \>
        Settings \> going to Cube Status \> tap "Refresh Cube" \> once
        connected, tap "Ping Cube" to light the Cube
        up]{style="font-weight: 400;"}
    -   [Alternatively, ]{style="font-weight: 400;"}[**change the
        battery**](#h_704656899181539002339381)
